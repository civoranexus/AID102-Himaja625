SoilSense is an AI-powered system designed to analyze soil health using key soil parameters and provide meaningful insights for better agricultural decision-making.

Why Soil Health Matters?

Healthy soil is the foundation of sustainable agriculture. By understanding soil conditions, farmers and researchers can improve crop yield, reduce waste, and make informed decisions.

Key Parameters Analyzed:

  • Nitrogen (N)
  • Phosphorus (P)
  • Potassium (K)
  • Soil pH
  • Moisture Level
