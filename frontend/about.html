About SoilSense

SoilSense is an intelligent soil health analysis platform developed as part of the CivoraX Internship Programme. The project focuses on transforming raw soil parameter data into meaningful insights that support sustainable agricultural practices.

The system evaluates essential soil parameters such as Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Potassium, pH, and moisture levels to determine overall soil condition. Based on this analysis, users receive clear health classifications and advisory suggestions.

SoilSense currently uses rule-based decision logic and is architected to support future integration of machine learning models for predictive soil health analysis, crop suitability recommendations, and smart irrigation advisory systems.

This project emphasizes clean design, clarity, and scalability, laying a strong foundation for advanced agricultural intelligence solutions.
